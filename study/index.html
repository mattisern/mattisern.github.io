Overview

Between January 5th 2026 and April 30th 2026 we will go through the Eight Gates of esotericism together. We will study different traditions of the hidden teachings to learn the techniques needed to shape reality.

  1. Metaphysics
  2. Personal myth: the hero's journey
  3. Energy systems
  4. Healing & miracles
  5. Creation
  6. The flow of Tao
  7. Alchemy
  8. Rituals & ceremonies

We will translate occult knowledge into practical terms of creativity, health, relationships, and our Great Work.

Format

  • A balance of hermetic theory and practical applications.
  • Weekly check-ins with your fellow practitioners.
  • A private, encrypted chat for reflections in the group.
  • Shared notes to gather our learnings into a Book of Keys.
  • Access to 1-on-1 sessions for extra support.

If you are interested in in-person retreats and 1on1s, that will be available later in the year.
