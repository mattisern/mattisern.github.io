Overview

Between January 5 2026 and April 30 2026 we will go through the Eight Gates of esotericism together. We will study different traditions of the hidden teachings to learn the techniques needed to shape reality.

  1. Metaphysics – structure of higher realities
  2. Personal myth: the hero's journey
  3. Energy systems
  4. Healing & miracles
  5. Creation
  6. The flow of Tao
  7. Alchemy
  8. Rituals & ceremonies

We will translate occult knowledge into practical terms of psychology, relationships, and our own Great Work.

Format

  • Shared notes to gather our learnings into our Book.

If you are interested in in-person retreats and 1on1s, that will be available as well later in the year.
