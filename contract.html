Participant Agreement

The Eight Gates – A Guided Exploration of the Esoteric Arts

Effective as of the date of the last signature below
Course Start: January 5, 2026

This Participant Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into between Mattis Erngren (“Facilitator”) and the undersigned Participant. By enrolling in The Eight Gates – A Guided Exploration of the Esoteric Arts (the “Course”), the Participant agrees to the following.

1. Purpose and Scope

  • The Course is an exploratory study program focused on practical and philosophical aspects of the esoteric arts—alchemy, hermeticism, metaphysics, and higher-self communication.
  • The Facilitator’s role is to hold the structure and share readings and exercises as a fellow practitioner.
  • The Course is not therapy, medical treatment, or initiation, and no guarantees of specific outcomes are offered.

2. Health & Psychological Responsibility

2.1 Not a Substitute for Therapy
The Course is offered for educational and spiritual exploration only. It is not intended to diagnose or treat any condition.

2.2 Participant’s Responsibility
The Participant affirms that they are in stable psychological condition and will seek professional support if needed. The Facilitator may, at discretion, request confirmation from a licensed professional if suitability is uncertain.

2.3 Removal for Safety
The Facilitator may remove a Participant if participation appears harmful to themself or the group. A proportional refund will be issued for remaining sessions.

3. Participation & Commitment

3.1 Contribution
Participants contribute 888 us dollars, paid securely through Stripe Checkout to the Facilitator. The contribution represents commitment to presence, not payment for guaranteed results.

3.2 Payment & Confirmation
Payment is made via Stripe Checkout using a payment link provided by the Facilitator. Enrollment is confirmed automatically once payment is completed. Participants are expected to complete payment no later than December 20, 2025.

3.3 Refund Policy
A full refund is available until December 29, 2025. After January 5, 2026, refunds are not provided except under § 2.3.

3.4 Missed Sessions
Missed group sessions are not refundable. Participants may access internal notes or recordings if provided.

4. Confidentiality & Group Conduct

4.1 Privacy
All personal sharing within the group is confidential.

4.2 Recording
No recording, screenshots, or redistribution of materials without written consent.

4.3 Respectful Behaviour
Participants must maintain respect and goodwill. Disruptive or harmful behaviour may result in removal without refund.

5. Intellectual Property

All written, audio, and visual materials created by the Facilitator remain the property of Mattis Erngren. Participants receive a limited, non-transferable licence for personal use only.

6. Logistics & Communication

  • One group session per week (online) and optional 1-on-1s.
  • Communication by email; responses within 48 hours.
  • Time zone: Central European Time (CET/CEST) but flexible – we will find a time that works for all parties.

7. Governing Law & Data Protection

This Agreement is governed by Swedish law. Any disputes fall under Uppsala District Court.
All personal data are handled in compliance with the EU GDPR.

8. Entire Agreement

This document contains the full understanding between the parties and supersedes all prior communication. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both.
